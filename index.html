How to Use

  1. Select Recording Sources

    Choose to record your screen, camera, microphone, or system audio.

  2. Start Recording

    Click the record button or use the keyboard shortcut ⌘⇧P.

  3. Control Recording

    Pause and resume with ⌘⇧X, or stop with ⌘⇧C.

  4. Save & Download

    Save recordings locally and download them anytime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ScreenRecord.in free to use?
Yes, ScreenRecord.in is completely free to use. No registration or payment required.
Do I need to install any software?
No, ScreenRecord.in runs entirely in your browser using the MediaRecorder API. No installation needed.
What browsers are supported?
ScreenRecord.in works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
Where are my recordings stored?
Recordings are stored locally in your browser's storage. They remain on your device until you delete them.
What video format is used?
Recordings are saved in WebM format, which is widely supported and produces high-quality video files.
