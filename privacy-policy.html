Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ScreenRecord. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our screen recording application.

2. Data Collection

Local Processing: ScreenRecord processes all recordings locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your screen recordings to any external servers.

Local Storage: Your recordings are stored only on your local device using the browser's storage APIs. You have full control over your recordings.

No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact lists.

3. Permissions

ScreenRecord requires the following permissions to function:

  • Screen Recording: To capture your screen activity as you record.
  • Microphone (optional): To record audio if you choose to include it.
  • Storage: To save your recordings locally on your device.
  • Camera: To record video from your webcam if you choose to include it.

4. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device, your recordings never leave your device unless you explicitly share them. We implement no additional data transmission or external storage.

5. Third-Party Services

ScreenRecord does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. Your data remains entirely on your device.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your recordings. You can:

  • Record, save, and delete recordings at any time
  • Clear all local storage through your browser settings
  • Revoke app permissions at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the application's support channels.
